Security and safety online is important. Children should know how to make smart decisions online to always keep themselves safe. If your child is informed then you can feel at ease and not worry about your child’s presence online, trusting them to make the right choices.

It is important that your child understands the boundaries of using their device so as a parent, you should establish rules around the hours they should be using their device and where is appropriate to restrict this usage. Be a role model for your child! Follow your own rules and your child will follow them too!

Be aware of...

Privacy settings are a vital thing to be aware of. Have a look at 'settings' on your phone or in apps and look for the term 'privacy.'

Always ensure that location settings are turned off, similar to privacy settings, look for the term 'location services' in the settings on your device or in apps.
