Play Today!

Step #1

Choose an activity to take part in with your child to play the cards from our suggested list below. Choose a deck relating to a topic you want to talk to your child about. Each deck has a different card for communication, privacy and screen time.

Step #2

Download the cards at the bottom of this page or play online. Flip over the next card for a clue! Explore with your child together to find the clue.

Step #3

When your child finds the clue, ask the question on the back of the card and discuss it. Flip over the next card for another clue and follow the same steps mentioned in steps #1 and #2.

Step #4

You can use these cards as conversation starters to bring up a touchy subject. Congratulations! You are now on the way to communicate about Cyber Safety.

Inspect, connect
and protect.

Take part in these activities to create bonding time with your child. This will aid in the creation of a subconscious connection and open relationship! Connection and bonding are the key to communicating and understanding your child. These activities that we suggest are free, fun for you and your child and will create a experience outside of your normal everyday life and away from technology, which will lead to a more open relationship.

You could take
part in...

Go for a walk, get some fresh air. Take a walk along the river, in the forest or browse the city. Spend some bonding time with your child instead your devices.

walking

Climb a tree, hike a mountain, take on a hill walk and adventure along the way. Get out in the fresh air and experience something new together!

climbing

Exploring is a great bondingtool to get out of your every day envrironment and get active together; creating quality time, being a team and discovering new things.

exploring

Take the opportunity to make a family day and do or see the things on that unfinished to do list while spending time with your child and ensuring they're being safe online at the same time.

sight-seeing

Creating together is the perfect opporunity to build a bond with your child and create trust, an easy way to start the conversation by making and doing together.

arts and crafts

Similar to arts and crafts, building things while building a bond. Doing simple activities together creates trust and leads to naturally more open conversation.
